    Sajek vally

    The name of Sajek vally came from the Sajek river that originates from Karnafuli river. The Sajek river works as a border between Bangladesh. and India. Sajek Tripuri Vally is one of the most popular tourist spot in Bangladesh situated among the hill of the Kasalong range of mountains in Sajek union, Bagaicchari Upazila in Rangamati District. The vally is 2,000 feet above sea level. Sajek Tripuri vally is known as the Queen of Hills and Roof of Rangamati. The native people of Sajek vally are ethenic minorities. Among them chakma, maema, tripuri, pankho, kaibarta, lushai, other indegenous assamese communities, and Sagma are mentionable. Women seem to be more involved in economic activities here. Tea stalls , food joints and roadside market places are dominated by women. Picking fruits and vegetables early in the morning is a common trade here.They are not fluent in Bengali but young population speak some English.

    Saint martin

    There are many tourist spots in Bangladesh. Saint Martin’s, Cox’s Bazar, Srimangal, Madhabkundu etc. are special tourist places of our country. Saint Martin’s island is the most beautiful one of them. It is the coral island in Bangladesh. We can see beautiful coral of different shapes and colours in the blue waters of the Bay of Bengal. We can also see the turtles and different kinds of sea-fishes. Saint Martin’s island is about 26 nautical miles from tecnaf. We can go from Chittagong to Tecnaf and then Tecnaf to Saint Martin’s Island by sea-bus or traditional wooden boats. The trips are exciting but safe. The natural scenery of Saint Martin’s is so much beautiful.There are many tourist spots in Bangladesh. But the main tourist spots of Bangladesh are Kuakata sea beach, Sundarban, Cox’s Bazar, Tea garden at Srimangal, Saint Martins Island etc. Saint Martins Island is one of the best tourist spot. Saint Martin’s Island is the only coral island in Bangladesh. It is a small island in the North-Eastern part of the Bay of Bengal. The local name of the island is “Narikel Jinjira”. The island is enriched with coral of different shapes and colors and the sea turtles. The people of the Island depend largely on fishing for livelihood, as there is a variety of sea fishes. The island has become a popular tourist spot. It is 26 nautical miles away from Teknaf. One can go there by sea bus.

    Green Vally Park

    Green Valley Park is located at Lalpur Upazilla which is 40 km away from Natore district town. The distance of the park from Lalpur Sadar is only 1 km. The park has been built for fun and entertainment in a beautiful and pleasant environment. Built on about 123 bighas of land, the park has been decorated with various recreational facilities. From children to adult, this park is a great place for all ages. A nice communication system has been founded in the village surrounded by the park. In the secluded environment of the park, anyone will find recreational as well as breathtaking natural beauty. The park has various rides for recreation. There are speed-boats, paddle boats, bullet trains, mini trays, nagordola, pirate ships, mari-goround, honey swings and more. The whole park has been decorated with various types of flowers. The whole park is full of different types of fragrance. There is a beautiful lake spreaded over about forty acres of land. Anyone can float in the water of this beautiful lake through speedboat. Here anyone will find everything to entertain in a very pleasant environment. There are many advantages here including Prayer Arrangements, Picnic Spots, Shooting Spots, Adventure Rides, Concert & Playgrounds, Own Power Facilities, Security Services,Decorator Facilities, Car Parking, Cafeteria Shop Corner, Meeting-Seminar Venues, Accommodations. It is a great place as a picnic spot for educational institutions, various organizations and institutions. Besides, various toy and jewelery shops have been set up near the park. Keeping in mind the beauty and all the facilities of this tidy park, anyone can put the park on the list of favorites for any event. The fixed entrance fee for visitors is Tk 50/- per person. The park is open from 9 am to 5 pm every day. However, this time changes with the change of seasons.
